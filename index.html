Since there will be high demand for this service, we cannot deliver all messages.
To increase the chances that your message will be selected.
1) Make sure it is below 140 charachters (under about 30 words)
2) It is hilarious, fun, and simply awesome.
3) It is NOT offensive or hateful.

How it all works

1) We choose a funny and simply awesome message to be delivered from those submitted.
2) We elegantly hand write it in Harnwell.
3) We send you a photo of it to your phone or email.
4) We then send the message over via Zipline from Harnwell to Rodin.
5) We send you another photo of the message being received in Rodin.
6) Once the message is in Rodin, we deliver it to your friends addressed room.
7) We take a picture of your friend holding the message (or slip it under the door if they are not in the room)
8) Rinse and Repeat!
